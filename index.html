Essential Mongolian Phrases

Pronouncing basic Mongolian words goes a long way when staying with nomadic families. Try these:

Greeting

Sain baina uu?

Literal: Are you doing well? (Universal Hello)

"Sighn Bain-oo"
Gratitude

Bayarlalaa

Literal: Thank you

"Bah-yar-lah-lah"
Entering a Ger

Nohoi khorio!

Literal: Hold the dog! (To call out upon arrival)

"No-hoy Hor-yo"
Asking directions

Хаана вэ? (Haana ve?)

Literal: Where is it?

"Hahn-ah weh"
